About KEXI

KEXI CONSULT Ltd is an accounting and advisory firm offering a broad range of expert professional services in accounting, tax and general business support including company registration and business plan development. KEXI Consult Ltd currently operates in Rwanda and was founded and registered on 28th September 2016.


Our Vision

Our vision is for KEXI CONSULT Ltd to be a nationally recognized and reputable professional service organization that offers outstanding client service, attracts and retains the best and brightest professionals, and is founded on shared values of professional growth, profitability and customer satisfaction.
